Important implementation note

This page is a polished policy template for the myVPN website and should be reviewed and adjusted with your final business, legal, infrastructure, analytics, and support details before production use.

1

Information we may collect

Depending on how the service is configured, myVPN may collect limited categories of information needed to operate the app, deliver support, maintain security, and improve reliability.

Account and support information

If you create an account, contact support, or submit a request, the service may process details such as your name, email address, subscription status, device platform, and the content of your message.

Technical and service information

The app or website may process diagnostic information such as app version, device type, crash data, language, region, approximate timestamps, and limited connection metadata required to operate and secure the service.

If the product follows a strict no-log architecture for browsing activity, that claim should be reflected here only when it matches the real production design, infrastructure, and retention practices.

2

How information may be used

  • To provide VPN connectivity, app functionality, and account access
  • To maintain uptime, troubleshoot incidents, and prevent abuse
  • To respond to support requests and service inquiries
  • To process subscriptions, billing status, or restore purchases when applicable
  • To improve product performance, usability, and reliability
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce service terms
3

Sharing and disclosure

myVPN should not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, fraud prevention, payment processing, infrastructure operations, analytics, customer support, or a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

If third-party vendors are used, they should be listed or described with enough clarity to explain their role in app delivery, payments, crash reporting, analytics, email communication, or customer support.

4

Security and protection measures

myVPN is expected to use technical and organizational safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information it handles. These may include encrypted transport, access controls, least-privilege access, infrastructure monitoring, secure storage, logging for security events, and internal procedures for incident response.

No system can guarantee absolute security, but the service should be operated with a clear, security-first approach designed to reduce risk and protect users.

5

Data retention

Information should be retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or enforce agreements. Retention periods should match the actual systems used by the product, including billing, support, analytics, and operational logs.

6

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent for certain processing activities.

To exercise applicable rights, users should be able to contact the privacy or support team using the details listed below. Identity verification may be required before a request is completed.

7

Children’s privacy

myVPN is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service independently. The product should not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable legal requirements.

8

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, infrastructure, vendors, legal obligations, or product features. The effective date at the top of the page should be updated whenever material changes are published.
